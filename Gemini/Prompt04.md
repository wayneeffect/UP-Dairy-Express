## The Automated Delivery Pipeline

1. **Customer Setup & Address Verification:** Triggered once at sign-up.
The customer visits the website, enters their address, and selects their delivery cadence (e.g., weekly, bi-weekly). The software checks their location against your cousin's existing UP delivery boundaries and automatically assigns them to his established route day for that town (e.g., Newberry on Tuesdays, Manistique on Thursdays).


2. **Autopay & Order Lock-In:** 48 hours before delivery.
The website automatically charges the customer’s card on file. The system "locks" the order so changes can't be made last-minute, giving your cousin a precise count of what needs to be fulfilled.


3. **Automated Wholesale Manifest:** 36 hours before delivery.
The website aggregates every residential and business order for that specific route and exports a master "Pick List." Your cousin uses this list to pull exactly the right amount of extra inventory (gallons, half-and-halfs, cheeses) from his wholesale distributor/dock.


4. **Smart Route Optimization:** Morning of delivery.
The software syncs with a mobile app on your cousin's phone. It takes his core commercial store stops, injects the residential/office porch drops, and plots the most fuel-efficient, turn-by-turn driving sequence.
